Ridetime mobile app

Transit information on the go

Ridetime allows public transit agencies to publish bus and train schedules. From the riders perspective, Ridetime is a light-weight way to find out when the next bus or train is arriving.

Launch View

White label options allow custom branding for agencies

Main View

Although it is easy for first time users, Ridetime prioritizes the regular commuter persona, prompting recent and nearby stops to save retyping.

Ridetime employs a search-centric navigation scheme. The search returns destinations, stops, and routes. Autocomplete limits the amount of typing. With regular use, we will suggest trips and routes based on time and location. I.E. if the rider is around a work location at 5pm on a weekday, we will present the fastest trip home.

Trip View

A trip is a series of steps that can include multiple modes of transportation. Common trip plans can be saved as a favorite. The trip view presents the series of steps and transfers to get to a destination.

Route View

See buses as they move down the route. Updating time estimates until their next stop.

Regular transit riders often ride the same few routes. By visualizing these routes with real-time bus location, riders can see at a glance how far out a bus is to a given stop.

Stop View

Immediately see which buses are arriving or departing next and from which gate. Quickly view tailored list of the riders favorite routes.

During peak times there may be more than one bus or train route servicing a rider’s destination. The stop view provides an overview of all upcoming departures, and a subset of the rider’s favorited routes.
